# Tensara Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** Dec 19, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Tensara (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

- Account information (username, email)
- User Content, including code submissions, blog posts, and comments

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

- IP address
- Browser and device information
- Usage logs (page views, execution events)
- Execution metadata (timings, memory usage, hardware type, runtime environment)

---

## 2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Provide and operate the Service
- Execute, benchmark, and analyze submitted code
- Display leaderboards, rankings, and performance metrics
- Improve system performance, security, and reliability
- Detect abuse, fraud, or misuse
- Produce **aggregate, anonymized, or statistical analyses** for:
  - research papers
  - technical blog posts
  - academic publications
  - talks and presentations
  - educational or marketing materials

---

## 3. Public Information

The following information may be publicly visible:

- Username
- Public submissions and associated performance metrics
- Leaderboard rankings
- Comments and community contributions

Hidden or private submissions are not shown to other users but may still be used internally and in analyses as described above.

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share information:

- With infrastructure providers (hosting, GPU providers, analytics)
- To comply with legal obligations
- In aggregated or anonymized form that does not identify individuals

---

## 5. Data Retention

We retain data as long as necessary to operate and improve the Service, comply with legal obligations, and support research and benchmarking.

Deleted accounts may still have retained submissions, benchmarks, and aggregate statistics.

---

## 6. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards. However, no system is completely secure.

---

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data.

Requests can be sent to **hello@tensara.org**.

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
